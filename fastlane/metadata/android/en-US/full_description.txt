ScryBook is a modern and clean Android application designed for writers and authors who want to carry their manuscripts everywhere.

Whether you are writing your first novel or polishing a complex work, ScryBook offers a distraction-free interface with all the tools necessary to manage your literary project.

Key Features:
- Rich Editor (WYSIWYG): A smooth writing interface with support for bold, italic, headings (H1-H3), lists, and images.
- Smart Auto-Save: Never lose a word. The app auto-saves every 30 seconds, during chapter transitions, or when closing the app.
- Complete Project Management: Organize your book into chapters, create detailed character profiles, and document settings.
- Desktop Compatibility: ScryBook uses the .sb format (SQLite database), ensuring complete compatibility with the desktop version.
- PDF Export: Generate a clean manuscript in PDF format directly from your phone or tablet.
- Speech-to-Text & Text-to-Speech: Listen to your drafts or dictate your thoughts on the go.
